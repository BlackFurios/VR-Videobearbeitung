Controls (Android|Windows):

A:	Pause video

B:	Show current time

Y:	Restart video

X:	Open StartMenu

L1:	Reverse video

R1:	Forward video

L2:	Creates highlight

R2:	1. Opens HighlightMenu of the selected highlight
	2. Activates options in the opened StartMenu (DropdownList, Play Video, Save)
	3. Activates the selected video in the dropdownlist of the HighlightMenu
	4. Activates options in the opened HighlightMenu (Connect, Disconnect, GetInfo, Delete, Close)
